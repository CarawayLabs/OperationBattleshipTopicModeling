This Digital Product Manager role is responsible for defining and iteratively enhancing our API Onboarding procedures as new products and new business models emerge.

As part of the team this role will collaborate on ways to drive enhancements to the overall customer onboarding experience.

This role must combine excellent organizational skills with an ability to drive multiple stakeholders to complete necessary tasks to ensure success.

Define strategy, roadmap and own refinements based on business objectives, customer research, competitive dynamics and collaboration with Product leads, lines of business and cross-functional partners.

Look for ways to improve customer onboarding process and experience, both from an employee and customer viewpoint, and act as a key stakeholder in coordinating this work across all teams to enable an ultimate launch date.

Perform acceptance testing of feature as needed and analysis supporting the strategies.

Partner with API Product Managers, engineering, and business line teams to define the process of onboarding and configuring customers to each API.

Assess all new products, platforms, environments, customer models, etc., and determine what Support Platforms, Support Processes, and Reporting must change to effectively support and provide metrics.

Ensure the API Product knowledgebase is updated by the Product Owner of the API product, to facilitate the launch, and ensure all support teams are informed.

Become the subject matter expert with in-depth knowledge of our API product onboarding process.

Oversees API Onboarding documentation, making sure it is accurate and current.

Provides customer experience feedback with all stakeholders.

Define metrics, run reports, and perform periodic audits to measure performance against best practices.

Recommend changes to platforms & processes for iterative improvement. Create and develop the partnerships to drive and deepen the relationships across the enterprise.

Define the enterprise standard best practices & governance for API customer onboarding. Create and align operational processes that involve multiple teams, train support teams & communicate operational readiness updates with requester / line of business stakeholders.

Become the subject matter expert with in-depth knowledge of our API product onboarding process to quickly facilitate customer inquiries as needed.
